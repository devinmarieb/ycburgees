Yacht & Boat Clubs

A collection of clubs and their burgees.

The site's collection is a work in progress - moving from east coast to west coast - creating a comprehensive list of yachting and boating clubs across the US.

  • See Clubs

Clubs are being added over time - starting with the east coast and moving west. If your state has been added and you don't see your club, there likely wasn't an official website (or Facebook account) or identifiable burgee. If you would like to add or remove your club, correct information, or send a higher resolution burgee image, email ycburgees@gmail.com.
